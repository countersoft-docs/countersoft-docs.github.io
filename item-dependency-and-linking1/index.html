Item Dependency and Linking

Every item can be linked or depend upon other items.

Linking

A link provides the means to associate two items.

You can link an item to multiple items and with no hierarchical relationship.

Dependency

Item dependencies provide a hierarchical relationship of parent/child.

A parent can have multiple children and can also can be a child of some other item.
